Kenney MB61161 Waterproof Shower Curtain Liner

Keep your bathroom clean and fresh with the Kenney MB61161 shower curtain liner. Engineered with Microban antimicrobial technology, it prevents the growth of bacteria, mold, and mildew, ensuring your shower space stays hygienic for longer. The eco-friendly PEVA material is not only waterproof but also free from harmful PVC, making it a sustainable and odorless choice for every day use.

Ideal for those with sensitive skin or who value a bacteria-free environment, this product is a reliable addition to your bathroom accessory collection. It combines practicality, style, and long-lasting performance for a better shower experience.
